About Red Box Software

Red Box Software is a small software development and IT company based in Tauranga NZ. We are a family run business that focuses on creating beautiful, powerful and affordable solutions for our valued customers. We care about you and your business and we want to help you succeed. We create websites and web applications, desktop applications, mobile applications and we also offer IT support and consulting services. We also provide cyber security services to help protect your business from cyber threats. We can also help you automate your business processes with Robotic Process Automation (RPA). With all these tricks up our sleeves we are confident that we can help you with your software and IT needs!
